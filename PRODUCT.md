# Catecismo Digital - PRODUCT.md

## Users
- **Students** (primaria a universitarios): Aprendiendo doctrina católica, progresando en su formación, realizando quizzes, participando en gamificación
- **Catechists/Teachers**: Gestionando clases, creando asignaciones, rastreando progreso estudiantil, comunicándose con padres
- **Parents/Tutors**: Monitoreando progreso de sus hijos, recibiendo notificaciones, coordinando con catequistas
- **Parish/Institution Admins**: Gestionando usuarios, viendo analíticas de participación, administrando recursos
- **Super Admins**: Control total de la plataforma, configuración global, gestión de todos los roles anteriores

## Brand
Catholic formation platform that combines deep theological education with modern, clean edtech aesthetics. The visual identity is **Notion-minimal premium edtech** — clean, functional, professional — with a **pastoral, sobriety** tone. 

Key brand attributes:
- Clear, profound, accessible Catholic teaching
- Modern, functional design without liturgical aesthetics
- No baroque elements, no gold gradients, no serif fonts, no children's illustrations
- Bilingual (ES/EN) with seamless language switching
- Focus on substance over ornamentation

## Tone
- Pastoral and sober (not cold or corporate)
- Professional yet approachable
- Respectful of the subject matter
- Clear and direct in communication
- Warm but not overly casual

## Anti-references
- Typical Catholic websites with dated designs, heavy ornamentation
- Corporate SaaS with excessive gradients, glossy effects
- Overly "cute" or childish religious education sites
- Dark mode academic tools that feel cold or uninviting
- Sites that use religious imagery as mere decoration without substance

## Strategic Principles
1. **Clarity first**: Catholic doctrine should be accessible and understandable
2. **Function over form**: Every design element should serve a purpose
3. **Respect the content**: Design should elevate, not distract from, the teachings
4. **Inclusive access**: Bilingual, mobile-friendly, accessible to all ages
5. **Premium simplicity**: High-quality, refined minimalism that feels substantial
